# Create a new team {#create-a-new-team}

If no team exists for your project in Experience Rollouts, you need to request one before you can start creating feature flags. Team creation is handled through a support request.

## Before you begin {#before-you-begin}

Confirm that a team does not already exist for your project. Check with your product or engineering lead, or review [Teams and their admins](teams-and-admins.md). If a team already exists, request to join it instead — see [Request access](request-access.md).

## Submit a team request {#submit-request}

Contact Experience Rollouts support to request a new team. Provide the following information in your request:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Team name** | A unique name for your team. |
| **Targeting mode** | The targeting mode your team will use: [user-level targeting or org and environment-level targeting](../../getting-started/experience-rollouts-modes.md). |
| **Environment** | The environment(s) you need: Stage, Production, or both. |
| **Team admin(s)** | Email address(es) of the person or people who will serve as team admins. |

>[!NOTE]
>
>Team admins are responsible for managing members and approving access requests. Designate at least two admins to avoid access gaps if one admin is unavailable.

## After the team is created {#after-creation}

Once your team is created, admins can begin adding members in a self-serve way. New members request access through the access management system and admins approve or reject those requests.

See [Request access](request-access.md) for the steps members should follow to join the team.
