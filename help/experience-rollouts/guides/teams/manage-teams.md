# Manage teams {#manage-teams}

Teams are the core organizational unit in Experience Rollouts. Each team owns one or more applications and manages all feature flags, feature groups, and releases for those applications.

To manage a team, you must have the **Admin** role. See [User roles](user-roles.md) for a full description of available roles and permissions.

## Team management tasks {#tasks}

As a team admin, you can perform the following tasks:

* [Add members to your team](add-team-members.md) and assign them the appropriate role
* Remove members who no longer need access
* Approve or reject incoming access requests
* Onboard new applications to the team — see [Manage applications](../applications/manage-applications.md)

## Team structure {#structure}

Teams in Experience Rollouts have a flat structure. All members with the same role share identical permissions — there is no sub-hierarchy within a role. Multiple roles can be assigned to the same member when broader access is needed.

## Creating a new team {#create-team}

New teams are created on request. See [Create a new team](../console/create-a-new-team.md) for the required information and steps to submit a request.
